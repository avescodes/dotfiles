A.8 XEP-0045 (Multi-User Chat)

jabber.el supports parts of XEP-0045. Entering, leaving and chatting work. So do invitations and private messages. Room configuration is supported. Changing roles of participants (basic moderation) is implemented, as is changing affiliations, but requesting affiliation lists is not yet supported.
