A.14 XEP-0066 (Out of Band Data)

jabber.el will display URLs sent in message stanzas qualified by the jabber:x:oob namespace, as described in this XEP. Sending such URLs or doing anything with iq stanzas (using the jabber:iq:oob namespace) is not supported.
