15.6.5 Info alerts

Info alerts are sadly underdeveloped. The message function, jabber-alert-info-message-function, takes two arguments, infotype and buffer. buffer is the buffer where something happened, and infotype is either 'roster for roster updates, or 'browse for anything that uses the browse buffer (basically anything except chatting).

The info alert hooks take an extra argument, as could be expected.
