A.24 XEP-0096 (File Transfer)

Both sending and receiving files is supported. If a suitable program is found, MD5 hashes of outgoing files are calculated and sent. However, hashes of received files are not checked. Ranged transfers are not supported. In-band bytestreams are not yet supported, even though XEP-0096 requires them.
